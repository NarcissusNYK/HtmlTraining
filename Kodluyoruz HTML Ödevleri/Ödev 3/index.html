* Spinach Gratin *

This easy makeover Spinach Gratin is creamy and decadent, with a hint of nutmeg baked in the oven topped with melted Gruyere cheese for a must at your Holiday table!

Prep:10 mins Cook: 40 mins Total:50 mins


Ingredients
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 3 cups milk
  • 3 lbs chopped spinach
  • 4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Instructions
  1. Preheat the oven
  2. In a heavy-bottomed saute pan melt the butter over medium heat. Add the onions and saut√© until translucent, about 10 - 12 minutes.
  3. Add the flour; cook 2 more minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the milk and cook until thickened, about 5 - 7 minutes.
  5. Squeeze as much liquid as possible from the spinach and add the spinach to the sauce.
  6. Add 1 cup of the Parmesan cheese and mix well. Season to taste, with salt and pepper.
  7. Transfer the spinach to a large baking dish and sprinkle the remaining 3 cup Parmesan on top. Bake for 20 minutes until hot and bubbly. Serve hot.
